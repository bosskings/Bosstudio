01 · Executive Overview

A vertically integrated content infrastructure hub

Boss Studios is a vertically integrated creative infrastructure company designed to serve the rapidly growing demand for professional content production and personal branding services in Port Harcourt, Rivers State.

The studio consolidates three complementary, revenue-generating divisions under a single, coordinated operational structure:

  • Grooming & Image Preparation Suite
  • Professional Photography & Visual Studio
  • Podcast & Audio-Visual Production Lab

The objective is to eliminate fragmentation in content creation by offering a seamless, premium environment where professionals can prepare, capture and record brand content in one location – from grooming, to still visuals, to long-form video.

Boss Studios seeks ₦35,000,000 in investment within a total capital requirement of ₦65,000,000. This document focuses on concept clarity, execution strategy and scalability. Detailed valuation discussions can be conducted in person.

02 · Market Opportunity – Port Harcourt

Under-served demand in a strategic city

Port Harcourt is a strategic commercial and political hub with strong concentrations of:

Entrepreneurs Oil & gas executives Political stakeholders Corporate managers Digital entrepreneurs Influencers & media personalities

Despite this concentration of high-value professionals, there is currently no fully integrated facility offering:

  • Professional grooming and image preparation
  • High-end photography and visual production
  • Dedicated podcast and audio-visual recording infrastructure

Content creation services in the region remain fragmented, with clients often coordinating:

  • Separate barbershops / makeup artists
  • Standalone photographers in rented spaces
  • Informal or improvised podcast / video setups

Boss Studios addresses this inefficiency by consolidating these services into a unified, premium creative ecosystem built specifically for authority positioning and brand storytelling.

03 · Operational Structure

3.1 Grooming & Image Suite

Purpose: Client preparation and brand presentation.

Core services include:

  • Precision haircuts tailored to professional image
  • Beard sculpting and maintenance
  • Professional makeup for on-camera readiness
  • Styling consultation for wardrobe and overall brand tone

Strategic value:

  • Enhances on-camera appearance and perceived authority
  • Increases average order value through grooming add-ons
  • Improves overall client satisfaction and repeat visits
  • Encourages bundled bookings (grooming + photo + podcast)

3.2 Photography & Visual Studio

Built for corporate, political and digital brand storytelling.

Infrastructure:

  • Cyclorama wall for clean, modern visuals
  • Professional strobe lighting systems
  • Softboxes and light modifiers
  • Adjustable backdrop systems
  • Dedicated corporate headshot station
  • Product photography and campaign area

Services:

  • Corporate branding portraits and executive headshots
  • Campaign visuals for political and corporate initiatives
  • Editorial and lifestyle brand shoots
  • Social media content batching sessions
  • Political campaign imagery and media kits

Revenue structure:

  • Hourly studio bookings
  • Half-day and full-day production packages
  • Bundled service offerings with grooming and podcast

3.3 Podcast & Audio-Visual Production Lab

Engineered for long-form authority-building content.

Infrastructure:

  • Acoustic soundproofing and controlled audio environment
  • Multi-camera 4K recording setup
  • Professional condenser microphones and mic arms
  • Audio mixing interface and monitoring
  • Dedicated video editing workflow and workstation

Services:

  • Interview-style podcasts and thought-leadership content
  • Corporate communications and internal media
  • Political campaign discussions and policy conversations
  • Panel sessions and round-table content
  • Livestream events and hybrid broadcasts

This division is projected to become the highest-margin revenue unit, leveraging repeat bookings, long-form content formats and high perceived value.

04 · Revenue Model

Diversified and scalable income streams

4.1 Direct Service Revenue

  • Grooming sessions and image preparation
  • Studio hourly rentals for photography and video
  • Podcast and audio-visual session bookings

4.2 Bundled Creation Packages

“Complete Brand Session” (flagship package):

  • Grooming & styling
  • Professional photoshoot
  • Podcast or video recording session
  • Curated and edited deliverables for digital platforms

Bundling significantly increases profitability per client visit and simplifies the buying decision for busy professionals.

4.3 Membership Structure

Monthly plans for:

  • Corporate clients and executive teams
  • Public officials and political actors
  • Influencers and media personalities
  • Entrepreneurs and thought leaders

Membership benefits:

  • Priority booking windows
  • Discounted rates on bundled services
  • Reserved studio hours and content planning support

4.4 Studio Dry Hire

Dry hire rental to:

  • Production companies
  • Independent photographers and videographers
  • Media agencies and creative teams

09 · Risk Mitigation

Structured downside protection for investors

  • Tangible asset value: High-quality equipment maintains resale value, providing a hard-asset base.
  • Diversified service model: Multiple revenue lines (grooming, photography, podcast, memberships, dry hire).
  • Recurring membership strategy: Predictable baseline revenue from monthly retainers.
  • Moderate fixed overhead: Disciplined operations and lean staffing in early phases.
  • Growing demand for digital authority: Structural trend towards personal branding and content-led positioning.
Boss Studios is designed not just as a studio, but as infrastructure for digital authority – providing recurring, high-value services to a concentrated cluster of decision makers in Port Harcourt.
